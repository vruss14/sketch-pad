SketchifyPro

A web-based sketch pad designed for anyone who likes to scribble.

What We Offer

Browser-based Functionality

This sketch pad from SketchifyPro works entirely within the web browser of your choice. No additional installation steps or subscriptions are required to use the sketch pad.

Cross-device Support

The SketchifyPro sketch pad can be used on desktop, tablets, and mobile devices.

Multiple Colors

Sketch a drawing with any or multiple colors of the rainbow.

Reset Functionality

Once you finish sketching one drawing, feel free to press the reset button to begin drawing something new!

Instructions

  1. Create a new drawing
    • To begin a new drawing, simply position your cursor anywhere within the sketch pad. The default color is blue.
  2. Change pencil color
    • Change your pencil color by clicking on one of the available colors shown beneath the sketch pad.
  3. Erase
    • Click on the eraser icon beneath the sketch pad to switch to the eraser. When you are finished, be sure to click the pencil icon again to continue drawing.
  4. Reset sketch pad
    • To reset the sketch pad, click the reset button. Please note that this action is not reversible, and the entire sketch pad will become blank.
